Ferris Wheels

A Ferris Wheel is one of the most iconic and beloved attractions in amusement parks and fairs around the world. With its circular structure and slowly rotating cabins, it offers breathtaking views, a touch of nostalgia, and an impressive feat of engineering.

Merry-Go-Round

A Merry-Go-Round, also known as a carousel, is a classic amusement ride loved by children and adults alike. With its elegant music, twinkling lights, and beautifully decorated animals, it combines nostalgia, craftsmanship, and fun in one spinning circle.
