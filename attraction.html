Most dangerous rides

Adrenaline junkies, this one’s for you. While most amusement park rides are engineered with safety as a top priority, there are a few that have gained a reputation for pushing the limits—whether it’s due to extreme height, insane speeds,or a chilling history of malfunctions. Buckle up as we take a ride through the most dangerous rides ever built. These rides test the limits of fear, thrill, and sometimes safety. Not for the faint-hearted or light-stomached.
