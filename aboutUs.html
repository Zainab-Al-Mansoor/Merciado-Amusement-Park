About Us – Where the Fun Never Ends!

Experience the thrill! Merciado Amusement Park is home to over 30 rides, including the gravity-defying SkyWhirl coaster, the family-favorite Pirate River Adventure, and our newly launched virtual reality zone. Whether you're seeking heart-pounding excitement or gentle fun for little ones, there's something for everyone.

Plan your visit with ease. General admission starts at $39.99 for adults and $29.99 for kids under 12. Discounts are available for seniors, groups, and online bookings. Annual passes are also available and come with special perks like free parking and early ride access.

Your safety is our priority. Merciado Amusement Park follows strict safety protocols on all rides and attractions. Trained staff, regular maintenance, and clear signage help ensure a fun and secure environment for all guests. Please follow all posted rules and height restrictions for each attraction.
